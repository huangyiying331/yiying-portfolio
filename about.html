About Me

I’m Yiying Huang, an undergraduate studying Philosophy at UCL. I’m especially interested in how philosophical ideas intersect with marketing, psychology, and management—for example, how ethics, decision-making, and theories of value shape brand strategy, consumer behavior, and organizational culture.

This site is my portfolio. Here you’ll find selected projects and notes where I explore these crossovers in a practical, approachable way. Thanks for stopping by—feel free to look around and get to know my work.
