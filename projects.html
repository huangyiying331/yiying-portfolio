Stock Time Series Forecasting (2018–2024, Weekly)

Case: ICBC (1398.HK), Weekly Data · 2018–2024

This study analyzes weekly stock data of ICBC (1398.HK) from 2018 to 2024, comparing multiple statistical and machine learning models to examine their suitability, differences, and complementarity in short-term forecasting and structural analysis of stock price dynamics.

Data & Preprocessing

The raw price series was transformed into log returns to reduce non-stationarity and improve modeling accuracy.

Structural Analysis

Market regimes were explored through clustering and hidden state modeling. Rolling-window K-Means captured local structural shifts, while Hidden Markov Models (HMM) inferred latent market states. The temporal distribution of states revealed transitions in market dynamics.

Predictive Modeling

Logistic regression was employed to evaluate the statistical relationship between historical features and the future price direction. Results showed explanatory power in capturing directional tendencies.

Technical Indicators & Strategy

A composite strategy was built using RSI, moving-average crossovers, and Bollinger Band width. By conditioning on technical signals, next-week return probabilities were estimated to identify statistically advantageous buy zones.

Tech Stack: Python · pandas · numpy · scikit-learn (K-Means, Logistic Regression) · hmmlearn · statsmodels · matplotlib
